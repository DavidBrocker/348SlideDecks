My Teaching Style

What Can You Expect?

  • Humor

    • I (try) to be funny, I think it makes the class more enjoyable!

  • Real-World Examples

    • We will use a mixture of real and fabricated data to help illustrate ideas and to show how you what we are discussing relates back to your current and potentially future experiences.

  • Interactive

    • Whenever possible, I favor dynamic content over static content. I want you to be able to see how statistical concepts look and move, rather than appear frozen in space.

  • Participation:

    • I want you to have practice outside of the classroom (low-stakes)
  • InQuizitive:
    • Textbook Component that measures critical thinking and understanding
  • Exams:
    • An adequate (not perfect) method of seeing how well you understand and can apply the material

What About AI?

Use It!

AI tools are everywhere and trying to avoid them or police their use is a bit of a fools errand! This is all I ask:

  • If you use an AI tool (LLM), show me how you used it!

  • Don’t let it replace your own thinking*

  • If you don’t know how to use it, ask me and we can go over it!

  • There is more than one tool:

    • ChatGPT

    • Claude

    • Perplexity

    • Gemini
